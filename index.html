The Civic Project

The Civic Project ensures uncomplicated access to all public services, utilities, elections, and forums for citizens of St. Croix Falls, Wisconsin.

About

The Civic Project was created out of a need to update rural communities' digital infrastructure to ensure that public services, data, and opportunities are accessible to everyone. Public services and information are hard to find and inaccessible. This is especially true in rural communities that lack overall resources. St. Croix Falls, WI has a population of 2,000. The goal of this project is to successfully redesign the city’s current website based on community input and feedback throughout the design process. The Civic Project concept and design is the creation of Haley Prochnow and is the capstone project for her Masters of Graphic and Web Design degree from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design.
